Welcome to Our Family Day at Topsy Farms!

Join us for an unforgettable day filled with joy and adventure at Topsy Farms. Experience the beauty of nature, bond with adorable animals, and create lasting memories with your family!

Activities to Look Forward To:

  • Feeding and playing with baby sheep.
  • Exploring a wide variety of yarn for your knitting projects.
  • Sampling and buying locally produced honey.
  • Enjoying a scenic ferry ride to the island.
  • Unforgettable moments with the farm animals.
